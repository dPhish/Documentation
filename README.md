> All-in-One Anti Phishing Solution

## Overview

dPhish is an All-in-One Anti-Phishing Solution that addresses all phases of a phishing attackâ€”pre, during, and post. It offers capabilities ranging from phishing simulation & awareness to phishing detection & response, email gateway assessment, and hunting stolen credentials.

## Key Features

- Phishing Simulation: Simulates multiple attack vectors on both employees to assess their awareness and on the email security gateway to assess its phishing detection efficiency.
- Security Awareness Programs: Educate the employee via a culture-driven cyber security awareness platform.
- Phishing Detection & Response: Minimize phishing impact through automated analysis and response.
- Unified Reporting and Analytics: Gain full visibility into phishing risk with dashboards integrating simulations, awareness, and phishing detection & response.
